# Multimodal Inputs

## Physical Signals
- optical sensing output
- particle-related measurements
- temperature
- humidity

## Contextual Signals
- time of day
- season
- weather forecast
- rain and wind conditions

## Human Feedback
- subjective discomfort
- sleep quality
- perceived comfort

## Principle
No single signal is assumed to be fully reliable. The system relies on multimodal fusion of weak and partially reliable signals.
